What this does

Your collateral and debt amounts are never stored on the public ledger. Instead, cryptographic commitments (SHA-256 hashes) are recorded on the Midnight network as private state — visible only to you. A zero-knowledge proof certifies that your position is overcollateralized without revealing the actual values. Anyone can verify your health status through the public attestation below.

How the proof works

1. Commitment — your collateral and debt amounts are hashed with a secret salt: SHA-256(amount || salt). The amounts cannot be recovered from the hash.

2. Circuit — the attest_health circuit in private_lending.compact verifies collateral × 10000 ≥ debt × 15000 (≥ 150% ratio) inside a zero-knowledge proof, without broadcasting the values.

3. Attestation — the Midnight network records only the proof hash, the overcollateralization result, and the risk class. No amounts on chain.
